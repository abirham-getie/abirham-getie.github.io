IT Ticket Triage & Knowledge-Base Generation
A production RAG application that analyzes 100K+ enterprise records (68K+ IT support tickets and 40K+ work orders), clustering them into 175 topic groups with ada-002 embeddings in pgvector, then orchestrating GPT-4.1 across labeling, summarization, deduplication, and LLM-as-judge validation to auto-generate 40 knowledge-base articles from 73 critical gaps. Drug Safety Signal Pipeline
An end-to-end pipeline that ingests FDA adverse-event data and computes Proportional Reporting Ratios (PRR) across 74,885 drug-reaction pairs to surface safety signals, correcting for the fact that raw report counts track a drug's popularity rather than its risk. Built with dbt staging and analytics models, 23 automated data-quality tests, Prefect orchestration, and a Docker build that runs the whole flow with one command.

Financial News Sentiment Classification
An end-to-end NLP pipeline classifying 1.8M financial headlines, validating FinBERT pseudo-labels against the expert-annotated Financial PhraseBank at 97.2% agreement, then benchmarking five models (LogReg, BiLSTM, DistilBERT, BERT-Base, FinBERT) plus a soft-voting ensemble. FinBERT reached 95.7% accuracy and 0.96 weighted F1, beating the classical baseline by 15 points, with a Streamlit app for real-time multi-model comparison.

AI-Powered Image Retrieval
A content-based image search system on Caltech-101 that extracts 2048-dim ResNet-50 embeddings and indexes them with FAISS for fast nearest-neighbor retrieval, paired with an OpenCV pipeline that filters blurry and compression-degraded images before indexing. Embedding quality validated with t-SNE, and deployed as a Docker-containerized Streamlit app. Built during the Build Fellowship (Open Avenues Foundation).
